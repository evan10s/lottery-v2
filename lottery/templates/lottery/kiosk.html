Terms of Service

By signing up for and participating in the Thanksgiving Lottery ("Lottery"), you agree to these Terms and indicate that you have read and understood the Privacy Policy.

As a person using a Lottery-sanctioned automated submission device ("Kiosk") and/or submitting entries to one or more of the games in the Thanksgiving Lottery ("Participant"), you agree to abide by the following rules:

  • Participants in the Lottery may only use one access card to submit tickets using a Kiosk. Once the card is scanned and the Participant has entered his/her name, they may not submit any lottery numbers with another access card that is not their own.
  • Duplication of access cards is prohibited via any means. Lost access cards may be replaced at the discretion of the Thanksgiving Lottery Organizing Committee ("Lottery Administrators").
  • The usage of unsanctioned automated number entry methods is strictly prohibited. Lottery-sanctioned Kiosks are exempted from this rule. Abusing access methods provided via Lottery-sanctioned kiosks, including setting up unauthorized Kiosks, is strictly prohibited. Additionally, tampering with equipment related to the operation of Lottery-sanctioned Kiosks, including network infrastructure, barcode scanners, and cables, is prohibited.

    Violations of these rules will result in disqualification from the Lottery. Violations may be detected and/or responded to via manual and/or automated rules.
  • Fraudulent ticket submissions by any means are prohibited.
    • When paper modes of submissions are provided, Participants are strictly prohibited from making submissions on behalf of another Participant. Upon discovery or reporting of such an occurrence, the affected submissions will be invalidated, and the Participant who submitted the fraudulent tickets will be disqualified from the Lottery.
    • Usage via any means of another participant's access card or an additional access card beyond the first is prohibited. Tickets and Scratchoffs submitted in this manner will be invalidated, and the violator will be disqualified from the Lottery.
  • Once a Ticket or Scratchoff is submitted, it cannot be modified. However, the Lottery Administrators may, at their discretion, invalidate and remove from consideration any Ticket or Scratchoff deemed to be submitted via any method in violation of these Terms.
  • Lottery results are determined using an automated process once the Lottery has ended. To be counted during the scoring process, tickets must be submitted during the ticket submission window set by the member(s) of the Lottery Administrators. The final ranking will be determined using the following criteria:
    1. Not disqualified
    2. Score (percentage)
    3. Sum of number of lottery numbers and scratchoffs submitted
  • Once results are determined to be accurate at the discretion of the Lottery Administrator(s), they are final and may not be contested.
  • The Lottery Administrator(s) reserve the right to disqualify any Participant for any reason at their sole discretion, including but not limited to violations of these Terms of Service.
  • Participation in the Lottery is wholly voluntary. Winning is based solely on chance; the odds of winning (2 correct numbers chosen) are 1 in 105 for the submission of one Ticket with 4 numbers selected. There is a 64 in 315 chance of selecting one correct number.
  • You may also be offered a Scratchoff game to play. Scratchoffs may award 0, 1, or 4 points. Each Scratchoff adds 1.75 points to the Scratchoff points possible. Each Scratchoff entry has 4 chances of winning. There are three (3) drawings with odds of 1 in 16 to win 1 point. There is one (1) drawing with odds of 1 in 16 to win 4 points. All drawings are conducted in series in a random order. The maximum points of won drawings will be awarded.
  • You may not circumvent any limits put in place by the system, including, but not limited to: maximum number of numbers that can be chosen on a ticket, range of numbers allowed to be chosen, maximum number of tickets allowed to be submitted in a certain time period.
  • You may not tamper with downstream services relied upon by the Lottery, including but not limited to Random.org.
  • Only Lottery Administrators may access areas of the software designated for Administrators. This includes the Manage Lottery and Lottery Admin pages.

Violation of any of the above policies may result in sanctions against your participation in the Lottery.

Privacy Policy
